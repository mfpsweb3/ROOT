Trouble in Terrorist Town

Trouble in Terrorist Town (TTT) is a multiplayer gamemode being added to mfpsweb3. Set in a parody of Metagascar universe, the game is about a group of “terrorists” who have traitors among them, out to kill everyone who’s not a traitor. This is a collection of 10,000 unique players which will participate in our Clan Wars update to mfpsweb3, each player belongs to a clan which will be tied to a clan in mfpsweb3 game.
